Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Choose Men's or Women's services from the navigation, pick a style, then select your stylist, date, and time on the booking page.

Can I cancel or change my appointment?

Yes — visit "View Appointment" from the navigation to see all your bookings and cancel any of them.

Do I need an account to book?

You'll need to sign up or log in first — it only takes a minute and lets us keep your booking history handy.

What if I don't see an available time slot?

Try a different date or stylist — most days have multiple open slots throughout the morning and afternoon.
